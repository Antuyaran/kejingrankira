The Milky Way
2022.4
Galaxy fuses filigree enamelling with contemporary jewellery styles to create filigree enamel pieces that appeal to a younger audience. The earrings are asymmetrical, with a combination of a brushed metal cross and a black metal drop on the left, with the body of the black drop shaped into an abstract fluid shape by filigree silver and filled with a pink glaze, and a black metal round bead and silver metal tassel on the right, with the same filigree silver pink glaze on the metal bead as on the right. I hope that this work, which incorporates a time-honoured folk technique with contemporary design diversity and a bold colour palette, will make more young people curious about the art of filigree enamelling and increase their understanding of it.
