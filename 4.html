adidas GEN-BLUE 2022
2022.5
GEN-BLUE PROJECT: adidas Originals Brand Culture Empowerment Program 2022 is a joint initiative of adidas Originals, Shanghai Institute of Visual Arts and DeTao Education Group. GEN-BLUE 2022 encourages young design students to create their own creative design language with the unique perspective and energy of Gen Z, creating and interpreting stories on the theme of breaking the boundaries of identity, era, virtuality and reality, and embodying the power of originality to participate in and create the grand celebration of adidas Originals 50th anniversary.
The GEN-BLUE Spark Film Festival is an extension of GEN-BLUE 2022, aiming to showcase the video works of the students in GEN-BLUE 2022, share their creative thoughts and lead the audience to witness how this group of Gen Z creators are burning with their creative passion.
